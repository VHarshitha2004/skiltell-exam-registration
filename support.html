Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register for an online course?

A: Visit the registration page, fill out the required fields, and click the "Register" button.

Q: What information is required during registration?

A: You need to provide your full name, registration number, email address, and any additional details required by the course.

Q: How will I know if my registration was successful?

A: A confirmation message will be displayed on the screen and you will receive an email confirmation.

Q: Can I update my registration details later?

A: Yes, once registered, you can update your details through your dashboard.
